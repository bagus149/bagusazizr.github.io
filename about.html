Hey, I am Bagus
Data Scienctist

I was born in Manado, North Sulawesi, Indonesia. Since I was in senior highschool, I really liked Mathematics. Eventhough I was not as good as my other friend, but I was not bad. After my senior highschool, I took Mathematics class in my collage. The class felt so boring because at that time I really didn't know where I should take this knowledge in the future. Until I found a subject called Machine Learning in my 3rd year. So, I decided to took what I'd learnt into that field. My thesis was about NLP, I analyzed about the sentiment analysis of online class during the pandemic of Covid-19.

After my graduation, I looked for a data scientist job. But, I realized that I didn't have enough skill and portfolio to show off. So, I decided to teach Mathematics in one of the homeschooling in Manado for a year and also I decided to start a bootcamp to improve my skill about data science. After 9 months, I am graduated from the bootcamp and now I am ready to get a job in Data Scienctist position.
